About Us

Learn about our club's history and Lions International

Our Club

Lions Club of Bangalore Sankalpa was chartered in 2018 as part of District 317A. We meet on the 2nd Saturday of every month at 2:00 PM in Sankalpa, bringing together community leaders dedicated to service.

Charter Year: 2018

District: 317A

Club Number: 135597

Location: Bangalore, Karnataka

Lions International

We are proud members of Lions Clubs International, founded in 1917. With 1.4+ million members in over 200 countries, we are the world's largest service club organization.

Mission

To empower volunteers to serve their communities, meet humanitarian needs, encourage peace and promote international understanding through Lions clubs.

Vision

To be the global leader in community and humanitarian service.

Our Mission

To serve our local Bangalore community through dedicated volunteer efforts, focusing on vision care, health services, education support, and disaster relief while fostering fellowship among our members.

Our Services

Making a difference in our community through focused service areas

Vision Care

Providing eye examinations, glasses distribution, and cataract surgeries for underprivileged communities

Diabetes Awareness

Health screenings, education programs, and support for diabetes prevention and management

Hunger Relief

Food distribution programs, community kitchens, and nutrition education initiatives

Childhood Cancer

Support for children battling cancer and their families through financial aid and emotional support

Environment

Tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and environmental awareness programs

Education Support

Scholarships, school supplies, infrastructure development, and literacy programs

Get Involved

Join our community of dedicated volunteers

Membership Requirements

  • Person of good moral character in the community
  • Commitment to regular meeting attendance
  • Willingness to participate in service projects
  • Ability to contribute time and resources to club activities

Membership Process

  1. Find a sponsor who is an active Lions Club member
  2. Submit application for membership
  3. Attend club meetings as a guest
  4. Approval by majority of club's board of directors
  5. Induction ceremony and welcome to the club

Member Benefits

  • Fellowship with like-minded community leaders
  • Opportunities for meaningful community service
  • Leadership development and training
  • International network of Lions members
  • Personal satisfaction from helping others
